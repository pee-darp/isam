39  * - squared error is not robust
40  * - Blake-Zisserman, corrupted Gaussian and Cauchy functions are

42  * - L1 yields a stable minimum, the median, but is non-differentiable at the origin
43  * - Huber cost function is convex, combining squared near the origin, L1 further out
44  * - pseudo-Huber is a good alternative to Huber, with continuous derivatives of all orders
